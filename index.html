Who I am

My name's Mario Alvarez-Picallo, I'm a MSc student currently enrolled in the second year of the MPRI program. I do computery stuff, hence the github account. You'll probably notice that it's quite empty, but I expect to start filling it as soon as I start having time for pet projects.

What I know

I graduated from the Universidad Autonoma de Madrid in 2014, attaining the equivalent of First-class Honours degrees in both general mathematics and computer science. This means that, among other things, I have a working knowledge of programming, operating systems, machine learning, artificial intelligence and so forth.

My experience in the MPRI has also given me some knowledge of type systems, abstract interpretation, linear logic, category theory and similar theoretical fields.

What I like

I'm mostly interested in research, mainly on topics related to type systems, functional programming, static verification and program semantics. I'm particularly interested in the applications of modal logic to type theory.

I'm also interested in philosophy, mostly epistemology and ethics, although my expertise in these fields is nearly non-existent.
